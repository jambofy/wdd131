About Me

I'm Nyasha Ushewokunze, a technology entrepreneur passionate about solving business problems through innovative solutions. I'm currently pursuing a degree in software development and am especially interested in leveraging Python and Django to create impactful applications. I'm involved in building platforms that integrate social media, websites, and customer relationship management. I'm also diving into agriculture and renewable energy. One of my exciting projects is Quicker, a web app designed to make self-service processes smoother in supermarkets. In addition to my tech ventures, I'm a pianist and have served as a missionary in Ghana and Kenya. My faith plays a significant role in my life. My ultimate goals include running a software company, running an agribusiness, and earning degrees in both technology and agriculture.
